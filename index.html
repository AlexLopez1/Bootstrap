Introduction

Video games are fun to play, especially with other people. But many questions linger on in the minds of many. Like are there any real benefits for playing long hours? What happens when the player plays too much? What role do video games play in the modern world?

This website is for a Web Development class project. The website's creation started on December 10th, 2021. It will discuss from the physical and mental effects of video games to how it impacted society.

The Research

Playing video games have shown a small improvement to many functions the brain performs. Basic hand-eye coordination and fine motor coordination have shown to be improved. Educational games can help grow math and reading skills and it inspire creativity. Strategy games allow for the use of speedy analysis, logistics, management, planning, and decision making. Playing simple games can help imrpove the player's mood by making them feel more relaxed. Multiplayer games enrourage cooperating and teamwork and those games can develop into virtual social communities.
